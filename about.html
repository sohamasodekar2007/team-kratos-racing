Who We Are

Official Formula Student Team of PCET's PCCOE.

Team Kratos Racing is a nonprofit student organization working in the Incubation Center of PCCOE, Pune. We consist of 43 enthusiastic Engineering Undergraduates dedicated to designing, manufacturing, and racing high-performance formula-style cars.

We are proud to be the only Indian team in the Top 10 World Rankings (2023), securing Rank 7 globally.

Our Story

Our adventure began in 2015, when a few of us decided to form a Formula Student team with innovation in our minds and determination in our hearts. From our very first vehicle (heaviest in the event) to represent India at Formula Student Germany and Hungary, we have come a long way.

We have successfully built three Combustion (CV) and three Electric (EV) race cars. We are now carrying forward the legacy of PCCOE Motorsports, striving to be known not just for our engineering, but for our hard work and team spirit.

Global Footprint

We don't just race in India. We compete against the best in the world at:

  • Formula Bharat (India)
  • Formula Student East (Budapest, Hungary)
  • Formula Student Germany (Hockenheim, Germany)
